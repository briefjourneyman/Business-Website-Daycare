Our Story

Providing comfort, care, and a warm learning environment for every child.

Welcome to Dahir Family Childcare

Dahir Family Childcare is a state-licensed, home-based daycare dedicated to nurturing each child's unique personality, strengths, and curiosity.

Our mission is simple: create a safe, warm, and joyful environment where children grow emotionally, socially, and academically through guided play and positive engagement.

We believe children thrive when they feel loved, supported, and encouraged — and we build our program around those values every single day.
